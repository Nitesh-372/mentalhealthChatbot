I know it feels like you did something wrong — and that weight can be crushing. But let’s work through this, gently:

1. Acknowledge what happened – Accept your actions without self-attack.
2. Apologize or make amends if needed – This creates peace.
3. Challenge inner criticism – Mistakes don’t define your worth.
4. Talk about it – Shame loses power when shared.
5. Forgive yourself – Self-forgiveness is a form of healing.

You’re human. Growth is born from mistakes. You’re allowed to move forward.
