It sounds like you're going through a lot of tension right now. Anxiety can feel overwhelming, but you're not alone. Here's how you can manage it step by step:

1. Try grounding techniques – Use the 5-4-3-2-1 method (5 things you see, 4 touch, 3 hear, 2 smell, 1 taste).
2. Control your breath – Inhale deeply for 4 seconds, hold for 4, exhale for 4.
3. Challenge anxious thoughts – Ask yourself: Is this fear based on facts or assumptions?
4. Limit stimulation – Reduce social media and overstimulation when you’re feeling anxious.
5. Talk it out – Sharing your fears helps you reframe them.

Anxiety wants you to feel powerless. But the truth is, you're capable of calming your mind and regaining control.
