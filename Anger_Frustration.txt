I sense a lot of heat in what you're feeling — and that's okay. Anger is a sign something needs attention. Here’s how to manage it healthily:

1. Pause before reacting – Count to 10, breathe deeply.
2. Express it safely – Write, talk, or exercise it out.
3. Find the cause – Ask: What’s really triggering me?
4. Set healthy boundaries – Don’t let others cross your limits.
5. Redirect the energy – Use the adrenaline for something productive.

You deserve to be heard — and you can express your anger without hurting yourself or others.
