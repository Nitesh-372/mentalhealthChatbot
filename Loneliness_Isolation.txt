I’m really sorry you’re feeling alone. Loneliness can be incredibly painful. It helps to know you're not invisible — you matter. Here's how you can gently reconnect:

1. Reach out – Send a message to someone, even a small ‘hello’.
2. Join communities – Try online groups, clubs, or hobbies that connect people.
3. Talk about how you feel – Even just journaling can create clarity.
4. Connect with yourself – Practice self-love and do activities that comfort you.
5. Consider therapy – A therapist can help you rebuild emotional connections.

Your presence is valuable. Let’s take one step at a time toward connection.
