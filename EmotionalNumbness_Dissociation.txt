It’s hard when you feel disconnected — like life is happening around you but not to you. That numbness is a defense mechanism. Here’s what you can do:

1. Try sensory grounding – Hold something cold, taste something spicy, feel textures.
2. Keep a journal – Even a sentence a day connects you to emotion.
3. Engage in body movement – Dance, stretch, go on a walk.
4. Limit avoidance – Slowly reintroduce emotional reflection.
5. Seek therapeutic support – Dissociation can be worked through safely with help.

You are not broken. Your mind is trying to protect you. Let’s walk gently back into connection — together.
