1. Acknowledge your feelings – Don’t suppress them; instead, write about them or talk to someone you trust.
2. Do small activities – Even a walk or listening to music can help regulate your emotions.
3. Reach out for support – Talk to a friend, family member, or consider therapy if it feels too heavy.
4. Create a small routine – Structure helps during times of emotional chaos.
5. Practice self-compassion – Remind yourself that struggling is human, and you’re not alone.
