You’re being really hard on yourself, and that hurts. Let’s shift the lens a bit and build you up from where you are:

1. List your strengths – Even if it feels silly, write down what you're good at.
2. Limit comparisons – Your path is your own.
3. Say kind things to yourself – Out loud or in writing.
4. Celebrate small wins – Progress matters, even if it’s tiny.
5. Surround yourself with people who lift you up.

You're not broken. You're worthy of love, respect, and kindness — starting with your own.
